# Welcome to Humanoid Robotics

This comprehensive textbook covers physical AI, humanoid robotics systems, and ROS 2 development from fundamentals to advanced topics.

## Quick Start

Ready to begin your robotics journey? Start with [Chapter 1: Foundations](/docs/chapters/chapter-01/lesson-01-foundations).

## What You'll Learn

- **Kinematics & Dynamics**: Forward and inverse kinematics, motion planning, trajectory optimization
- **ROS 2 Architecture**: Nodes, topics, services, actions, and distributed systems
- **Simulation**: Gazebo, Isaac Sim, and physics-based modeling
- **Perception & Sensing**: Computer vision, sensor fusion, SLAM
- **Control Systems**: PID control, model predictive control, learning-based approaches
- **Hardware Integration**: Actuators, encoders, embedded systems

## Course Structure

Navigate through structured chapters, each building on previous concepts with hands-on examples and exercises.
